Google Media Hackathon 2019

Media Hackathon is a competition that plans and implements ideas within a limited amount of time under the theme of media content and services. The theme of the event is "How to Get to the Truth: Contextual Journalism." Its goal is to implement verification of fact check and fake news (false manipulation information) on contents, solutions to filter bubbles, and various ideas for identifying original sources and actively consuming news, and develop them into actual service prototype types.
